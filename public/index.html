What is Crypto currency ?

Crypto currency is a digital currency in which transactions are verified and records maintained by a decentralized system using cryptography, rather than by a centralized authority.

Who Invented Bitcoin ?

Satoshi Nakamoto is the name used by the presumed pseudonymous person or persons who developed bitcoin. Nakamoto stated that work on the writing of the code for bitcoin began in 2007. On 18 August 2008, he or a colleague registered the domain name bitcoin.org

How does a block chain work?

Think of the block chain as a distributed database where each database has a copy of every transaction. This means that the data is always correct as you have many more copies to verify against.
